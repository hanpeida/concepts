\begin{problem}

  Suppose $f(t) = t-6$ from $0 \leq t \leq 12$ gives the rate of
  change of the profits for a company over the last year, where $t$ is
  measured in months.  What can you say about the company?
  \begin{multipleChoice}
    \choice{The company is profitable.}
    \choice{Overall, the company hasn't made money this past year.}
    \choice[correct]{The company's profits were decreasing but now they are increasing.}
    \choice{None of the above.}
  \end{multipleChoice}
\end{problem}
