\begin{problem}

  For the first year, a certain positive quantity grows at 1\% per
  week.  During the second year, the quantity decreases 1\% per week.
  How does its value at the beginning of Year 1 compare to its value
  at the end of Year 2?
  \begin{multipleChoice}
    \choice[correct]{The quantity is smaller at the end of Year 2 than at the beginning of Year 1.}
    \choice{The quantity is the same at the end of Year 2 than at the beginning of Year 1.}
    \choice{The quantity is larger at the end of Year 2 than at the beginning of Year 1.}
    \choice{It cannot be determined without knowing the initial value.}
  \end{multipleChoice}
\end{problem}
