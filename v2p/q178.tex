\begin{problem}
  I begin the game by setting $x$ to be 1~billion.  Each turn, I
  choose to either subtract one million from $x$, or to cut $x$ in
  half.  I win if I can get to a negative number.  Can I win in six
  turns?
  \begin{multipleChoice}
    \choice{Yes}
    \choice[correct]{No}
  \end{multipleChoice}
\end{problem}
